Education Worldwide

 

Importance of Education

Education is the process of teaching and developing knowledge. In this present day and age, it is expected that education is a fundamental resource in every country. Throughout the years, most countries have been making a major progress in expanding the accessibility to education for individuals at any level.

Figure 1 is a donut chart that showcases how educated each region is based on the number of learning outcomes that the region provides. There is an association with the number of learning outcome and the economic status of each region.




Number of Learning Outcomes




Learning outcomes is an important aspect of education. It is a method of learning to guide the body of educators to set clear expectations of what it is needed to be understood for the purpose of the academy. Countries that are more richer tend to have better education outcomes than those countries that are less developed.

With countries slowly expanding their resources over the past few decades, the education industry is able have more accessibility to provide expected learning outcomes therefore an overall increase in numbers of learning outcomes in 2017 since 1985.

Average Years of Schooling Across the Globe

This map shows the average years of schoolings by all individuals across the globe. Total Enrolment to Higher Education

Every region in the world has seen a significant increase in gross student enrolment to higher education. The global accessibility to education has improved over the past few decades. North America had a signifcant much more higher number of total enrolment than other countries in the year 1985. In 2020, Australia has overtaken North America as the current region to be the highest in total number of enrolment to higher education.

The Impact of Literacy

Literacy is refered to as the ability to read and write to enable effective communication. In the earlier years, countries across the entire globe had a signifcant lower literacy rate than it is today. Factors that manifested the significant rise in literacy rate over the years include the higher accessibility and affordability of education in each region along with the continuing expansion and development within each country. Building literacy in the early stages of life can help students grasp the effective means to communicate with a clear manner in modern society.





Education enables individuals to develop critical thinking, directing ways of using logic to make decsions and create effective conversational skills when interacting with other people. Thus, enchancing stability in life.
